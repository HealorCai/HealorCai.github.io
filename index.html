Biography

I am Zetao Cai, currently an undergraduate student at the College of Control Science and Engineering, where I am specializing in Automation and also pursuing a minor through the Advanced Honor Class of Engineering Education (ACEE) at Chu Kochen Honors College. My academic and research pursuits are deeply rooted in the field of robotics, specifically focusing on the design of robotic systems, the integration of artificial intelligence with robotics, and the planning and control mechanisms within robotics. Given my profound interest and dedication to the field of robotics, I am keen on advancing my studies and research in this area in the future.

Interests

Autonomous Flying Robots Team—ZJUFAST

Realizing that many of my peers lacked opportunities for hands-on learning outside the classroom, I planned and established a discipline competition team for quadrotor drone competitions, aimed at helping more students engage with robotics technologies in practical applications. My initiative received support from faculty, and together with my classmates, we founded the Autonomous Flying Robots Team—ZJUFAST with the support of Prof. Fei Gao. I dedicated my spare time to providing advanced training for team members in UAV systems, dynamics modeling, planning and control. My efforts meant that I could pass on my knowledge and experience to a broader audience.
